Sherly Lisa Janvier Bergmans

Sherly Lisa Janvier (Lisa) is a junior User Experience Designer and Front-End Developer based in
Rotterdam, the Netherlands.
She is well versed in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, React, and C#. Lisa has a passion for
creating educational (web) applications and artefacts,
which are often focussed around educating children and young teenagers on becoming
responsible digital citizens. Lisa finds value in creating things that impact
the world for the better. Curiosity and dedication are what continue to drive her.
Besides Programming, Lisa has an interest in all types of creativve endeavours which include,
but are certainly not limited to sewing and designing clothing, writing, photography,
and cooking. More about this can be found in the segment "projects: other"
